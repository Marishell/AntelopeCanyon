Antelope Canyon is located in Page, Arizona within the U.S. Touristy hikes are usually not my vibe, but it's no surprise why this beautiful place is becoming increasingly popular. Aside from being surrounded by other well known parks such as Grand Canyon National Park and Zion National Park, Antelope Canyon is made of 2 canyons: Upper and Lower Antelope Canyon. Schedule your tour carefully and you may be able to exeperience both on the same day! Unfortunately, at this time this beautiful place is closed. The land is owned by the Navajo Nation and they will notifiy the public once it's okay to begin visting again.
